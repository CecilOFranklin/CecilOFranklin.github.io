## 1. Purpose

This document collection serves a dual purpose: (1) as a professional portfolio and curriculum vitae, and (2) as a public notebook for ongoing research and computational experiments.

## 2. Scope

Two primary research questions drive the investigative memoranda in this collection:

1. **What materials can only be manufactured in microgravity or vacuum environments, and what applications do they enable?** (Program CF/SMM)
2. **What is the minimum viable industrial stack required to sustain manufacturing operations on the lunar or Martian surface?** (Program CF/ISR)

## 3. Document Convention

All memoranda follow the numbering format CF/TYPE—YEAR—SEQ, where TYPE designates the document class (TM, TN, CR, or TP), YEAR is the four-digit publication year, and SEQ is a zero-padded sequential number.

## 4. Distribution

All documents in this collection are unclassified and approved for unlimited distribution unless otherwise noted.
